Privacy Policy

BuildTrack  |  Last updated: April 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to BuildTrack ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application BuildTrack (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

BuildTrack is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) on our servers.

The App may access or process the following on your device:

  • Internet access: Used to load content and communicate with any connected services you configure.
  • Local files: The App uses Android's FileProvider to share files within the app; no files are uploaded without your explicit action.

3. How We Use Information

Any data processed by the App is used solely to provide the core functionality of BuildTrack, including:

  • Displaying and managing your build or project tracking data.
  • Enabling communication with services you configure within the App.

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.

4. Data Storage

All project data you create within BuildTrack is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any remote database of user content.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries. If future versions include third-party services, this policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Children's Privacy

BuildTrack is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect any data processed by the App. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
